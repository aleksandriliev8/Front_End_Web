Important Concepts

Cell Theory
All living organisms are composed of cells and all cells come from pre-existing cells.
Photosyntesis
The process by which green plants and some organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods with the help of chlorophyll.
Natural Sections
The process through which species adapt to their environment as individuals with favorable traits survive and reproduce.
